Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is a historic monument in Mumbai built in 1924 to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a magnificent white marble monument in Agra built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Thanjai Periya Kovil

Thanjai Periya Kovil, also known as the Brihadeeswarar Temple, is a grand Chola-era temple built by Raja Raja Chola I in Thanjavur.
